Fire Rescue

Fire Department

  • The fire department provides firefighting and certain rescue services.
  • The fire department in the city of Atlanta is known as Atlanta Fire Rescue.
  • Atlanta Fire Rescue (AFR) covers 132 square miles within the city limits of Atlanta, and the department helps neighbouring cities and counties upon request.

Did You Know?

  • Carelessly cooking with an open-flame outside has been the cause of many house fires.
  • You can get more information on fire safety at Ready.gov.
  • If your hair or clothes ever catches on fire you should stop, drop to the ground, and roll around to extinguish the fire.

History of AFR

  • AFR was created in 1848 when the Atlanta City Council formed a committee to investigate a fire problem in the city of Atlanta.
  • The first fire station in the city, Atlanta Fire Company No. 1, went into service in 1851.
  • Until 1882, the department was made up of volunteers; firefighting was not a paid career.

General Fire Safety

  • Make sure your home has a working smoke detector in every level.
  • Make sure there is at least one fire extinguisher in your house.
  • You should have a fire drill in your house. Make sure your family has a planned escape route and a specific place you can meet outside in case there is a fire.
