Public Works

What Are Public Works?

  • Public works consists of a wide variety of services offered in the city which focus on maintenance, development, and management projects for the city.
  • Some services that are considered part of public works are: solid waste services, recycling services, transportation services, and construction services.

Transportation

  • Atlanta's Office of Transportation looks after and maintains the city's street network.
  • Maintenance of the street network is done by keeping track of and fixing traffic signals, street lights, street signs, street maintenance, and on-street parking.

Solid Waste and Recycling Services

  • Solid waste (garbage) services handle taking people's garbage and getting rid or disposing of it.
  • Recycling services focus on collecting people's recycled items and actually processing them so they can be reused.
  • You can recycle most items made out of paper, glass, cans, plastic, and cardboard.

Construction Services

  • The Department of Public Works is responsible for keeping sidewalks and streets in good conditions.
  • You can report streets or sidewalks that are in poor condition and can be dangerous to use to Atlanta's Department of Public Works.
