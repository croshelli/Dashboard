Fire Rescue

Fire Department

  • The fire department provides firefighting and certain rescue services.
  • The fire department in the city of Atlanta is known as Atlanta Fire Rescue.
  • Atlanta Fire Rescue (AFR) covers 132 square miles within the city limits of Atlanta, and the department helps neighbouring cities and counties upon request.

History of AFR

  • AFR was created in 1848, when the Atlanta City Council formed a committee to investigate a fire problem in the city of Atlanta.
  • It is well known for being one of the largest providers of pre-hospital care in the southeast.
  • EMS can give people basic life support and advanced life support.

Mission

  • The goal of the EMS is to take care of individuals who need care right away or to get them to a hospital where they can get help.
  • The kind of care or help people receive with EMS before they arrive to the hospital is known as pre-hospital care.

Core Values

  • Basic life support (BLS) can be used to take care of people with major life-threatening injuries or illnesses, such as someone who has had a heart attack.
